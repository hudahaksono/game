How To Buy

Become a Meta Troll
Player Now

Step 01

Create a Wallet

Get Metamask or your preferred wallet at no cost from either the app store or Google Play Store. For desktop users, acquire the Google Chrome extension by visiting metamask.io.

Step 02

Get Some ETH

Ensure there's a balance of ETH in your wallet to transition to $TROLL. If you currently lack ETH, you have the option to purchase it directly through Metamask, transfer it from an alternative wallet.

Step 03

Go To Unisap

Link up with Uniswap by accessing app.uniswap.org either through Google Chrome or the browser within your Metamask app. Establish a connection with your wallet, then insert the $TROLL token address into Uniswap, opt for Troll, and validate your choice.

Step 04

Switch ETH for Troll

Exchange your ETH for $TROLL. Enjoy the advantage of no taxes, eliminating the need to be concerned about purchasing with a designated slippage. However, it's advisable to consider using slippage during periods of market volatility.
